Esteemed Guests

2K26

Department of CSE — TGPCET  |  Nagpur, Maharashtra  |  April 2026

Honourable Dignitaries & Distinguished Guests,

On behalf of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Tulsiramji Gaikwad Patil College of Engineering and Technology, we extend our most sincere and respectful invitation to grace AIXPLORE 2K26 — our flagship 24-Hour Mega Hackathon & VISIONEXUS a Poster Presentation Competition.

This premier national event unites the brightest student minds to ideate prototype and present cutting-edge solutions across Artificial Intelligence, FinTech, Healthcare, Climate Action and EdTech. Your presence would not only honour our students but inspire them to pursue excellence beyond the curriculum.

The event is scheduled on April 9th & 10th, 2026 at the J.R.D. TATA HALL TGPCET. Inauguration commences at 10:30 AM on April 9th. A prize pool of ₹33,333 awaits the champions. We earnestly request your gracious presence at the Opening Ceremony and the Closing & Prize Distribution on April 10th at 12:00 AM.
